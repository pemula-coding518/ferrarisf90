01 / HYBRID EVOLUTION

The SF90 Stradale is the first ever Ferrari to feature PHEV (Plug-in Hybrid Electric Vehicle) architecture which sees the internal combustion engine integrated with three electric motors, two of which are independent and located on the front axle, with the third at the rear between the engine and the gearbox.

02 / AERODYNAMICS

A completely redesigned aerodynamic profile ensures unprecedented downforce and efficiency. The shut-off Gurney, a patented active system located at the rear, regulates the air flow over the upper body, reducing drag at high speeds with low lateral dynamics loads and increasing downforce in corners.
